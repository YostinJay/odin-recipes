Chicken Rice

Ah, Chicken Rice, one of my favourite singapore dishes. A cuisine that may seem simple but can be made in many different ways.

Ingredients

Hainese Chicken

  1. 3 lb whole chicken(1.3 kg), giblets removed
  2. ¼ cup kosher salt(60 g), divided
  3. 4 inch pieces fresh ginger, peeled and cut into ¼-inch (6 mm) slices
  4. 1 bunch fresh scallion
  5. 1 gal cold water(3.7 L), plus more as needed
  6. 2 tablespoons sesame oil

Hainese Rice

  1. ¼ cup sesame oil(60 mL)
  2. 2 tablespoons chicken fat, chopped
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  5. 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  6. 2 cups long grain rice(400 g), rinsed and drained
  7. 2 cups reserved chicken poaching broth(480 mL)
